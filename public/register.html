Prohibited Activities

Engaging in any of the following will result in immediate account suspension or permanent ban.
  • Placing fake or fraudulent orders with no intention to pay or receive goods.
  • Using the platform to waste time, spam, or disrupt services for other users.
  • Submitting false payment confirmations or fake transaction references (UTR).
  • Attempting to exploit discounts, referrals, or wholesale pricing through deception.
  • Harassing, threatening, or abusing staff, delivery partners, or other customers.
  • Using bots, scripts, or automated tools to interact with the platform.

Orders & Payments

  • All orders are subject to product availability and price accuracy verification.

Placing orders just to pass time or with no genuine purchase intent is strictly prohibited and will result in account blocking.
